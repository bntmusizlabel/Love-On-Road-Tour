Tour Cancellation Announcement

It is with profound sadness that we announce the cancellation of all remaining dates for the Love On Road Global Tour. After extensive medical consultations, we have made the heartbreaking decision to cancel the tour to prioritize Rohit's health and ensure his ability to perform in the future.

A Personal Message From Rohit and The Team

To our incredible fans around the world,

This is the most difficult message we've ever had to write. The Love On Road Global Tour was meant to be our greatest adventure yet - a celebration of music, connection, and the incredible journey we've shared with all of you. To have to cancel these shows breaks our hearts in ways we can't fully express.

Rohit's recent diagnosis of severe hearing deterioration and vocal cord trauma has left us with no choice but to halt all performances. His medical team has been clear: continuing now would risk permanent damage that could end his career. While this news has devastated us all, we must prioritize his long-term health and ability to perform in the future.

We understand the disappointment this causes for everyone who purchased tickets, made travel plans, and supported this tour. Please know that we explored every possible alternative before making this decision. The thought of disappointing even one fan keeps us awake at night, but we hope you'll understand this was truly our only option.

To our fans: Your support means everything. These refunds will be processed immediately - you shouldn't have to wait because of our unfortunate circumstances.

To the media: We appreciate your concern but must insist on privacy during this challenging time. Rohit will not be giving interviews or making statements as he focuses on his recovery.

With deepest regret and endless gratitude,
Rohit Kshetri and the entire Love On Road family

All ticket purchases will be fully refunded to the original payment method within 7-10 business days. For alternate arrangements or special circumstances, please complete the refund form below. Our dedicated team is working around the clock to process all requests as quickly as possible.
